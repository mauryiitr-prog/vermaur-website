About Vermaur Farms

Vermaur Farms is dedicated to bringing premium-quality rice, traditional food products, and authentic farm-fresh goodness directly from trusted Indian farms to households across the country. Founded with a vision to preserve India's rich agricultural heritage, we focus on sourcing carefully selected grains and traditional products that deliver purity, nutrition, and exceptional taste. Our commitment is to provide customers with food that is natural, hygienically packed, and produced with the highest standards of quality and safety. Our product range includes premium varieties of rice such as Kala Namak Rice, Sona Masoori Rice, Doodh Malai Rice, along with traditional foods like Dal Badi and other farm-based products. Every product is selected and packed with care to ensure freshness and customer satisfaction. At Vermaur Farms, we believe that great food begins at the farm. By working closely with farmers and maintaining strict quality standards, we strive to deliver authentic flavors, trusted quality, and a wholesome food experience to every customer.
